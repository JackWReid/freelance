Welcome to PureBody Health Sports Injury Clinic

Summary

At PureBody Sports Injury Clinic we follow a simple and effective consultation process known as the W-PLAN. This ensures your injury gets diagnosed and on the road to recovery as quickly as possible. We treat every client as if they were a professional athlete, after all why should your pains be treated any differently to a World Champion 100-metre sprinters? It shouldn't!

The Process

  1. We Listen so that we can get a full understanding of your injury.
  2. We Assess. We like to make sure we know exactly what is going on and nothing is missed.
  3. We Explain. It is important you understand what your body is thinking and we know how to hep you understand your body.
  4. We Treat. We take huge pride in our ‘hands-on’ treatments. It contributes to a huge amount of your recovery but we can’t hold your hand all the time.
  5. We Rehab. ‘Hands-on’ treatment is only part of a full recovery, the rest is rehabilitation. Using our fully equipped gymnasium, we make your rehabilitation fresh and trendy!

Sports Massage

Do you have a secret passion for pain? Don’t we all...

This deep tissue massage is perfect for ironing out lumpy muscle fibres and reducing tension in all areas of the body. It is great both pre and post exercise because it assists with increasing mobility around joints as well as the elasticity of muscle tissue. A sports massage isn’t just for the athlete, in fact we use this treatment for a high number of manual workers and desk operatives who suffer from postural problems. A sports massage can be viewed as an ‘MOT’ for the body. We use this as an opportunity to break problems down before they progress into something more serious.

At PureBody Sports Injury Clinic we strongly believe prevention is better than cure.

Rehabilitation

At PureBody Sports Injury Clinic we take a clients rehabilitation very serious in deed. This will be far from a generic set of exercises drawn using stick men on a photocopied piece of paper!

Our rehabilitation programmes are specific to each individuals injury and their current goal. We provide both on-site and at home rehabilitation programme to help you recover from that injury and to get you back fighting fit whether at the office, on a work construction site or on the golf course. We believe that rehabilitation constitutes for over 50% of your injury recovery and have some new and trendy skills to get the exercises done. From experience, boring or repetitive exercises do NOT get done and this leads to a longer recovery.

We provide START to FINISH rehabilitation for clients of all ages recovering from:

  1. Orthopaedic surgery
  2. Back pain and a weak core
  3. Posture dysfunction
  4. Ligament sprains and muscle strains
  5. Joint stiffness and immobility

Rehabilitation sessions can last for 30 or 60 minutes and will include a personalised written programme emailed to you for your personal records. If you are that person that needs a little nudge with their rehabilitation exercises and wants the speediest recovery possible let us help you now!
