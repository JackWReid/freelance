Pure Psycle (45 min)

Generally speaking Spin classes are low impact but high intensity training. Each instructor will push you at different levels but remember to work at your pace when you need. Some classes may use the heart monitor. Definitely a wake up class! Some classes have an extra 15min. Cardio training and intense leg work out. If you plan to take up outdoor cycling or have a cycle challenge in mind, this will get you saddle ready. This session is suitable who regularly spinor if you want to work out before work regardless of level.

Pure Body Sculpt (60 min)

This workout combines strength, stamina and flexibility – everything you need to sculpt your body. A pretty intense workout to improve overall fitness. Impact varies. This is full functional training. Losing weight and improving your whole body appearance with long lean muscle tone. Functional training at its best! This session is suitable for everyone who wants to intensify their functional, core, stability training.

Core Stability (60 mins)

Not just an abdominal workout. Although this will tighten and flatten the tummy. There are many other benefits:Tightens the abdominal structures involved in movement and improves the transfer of power to and from the extremities.Teaches the muscles to work together efficiently and effectively.Aids in the prevention of injury.Improves spinal and postural control while the body is still and in motion.Helps to stabilize and align the spine, ribs and pelvis of a person to withstand static and dynamic force.Can improve respiratory function. Facilitates proper distribution of weight and assists the body in absorption of force and transfer of forces.Realignment and postural positioning.

Pure Hell Circuit (60 mins)

This class varies weekly structured between muscle groups and cardio fitness. The use of the weight machines / studio & cardio rooms are compulsory and Chris will push you to your limit. You’ll be dripping but you’ll certainly have a lot of fun along the way. Really challenges your fitness level. Don’t be afraid to try it, everyone’s got to start somewhere! ALL levels catered for. This class is best for Intermediate to Advanced (beginners always welcome).

Body Box (60 mins)

Designed to work your aerobic and anaerobic fitness levels: This class will have you packing punches, smashing crunches and everything in between and it’s a great way to burn calories whilst having some fun. Working hard at the gym but fancy something different – give this a go! A definite stress buster!
