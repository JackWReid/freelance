A collective of friends, DJs, promoters and music-lovers, Mahatma was born out of a shared passion for live music and a desire to contribute to Exeter’s developing nightlife. In the longstanding tradition of British club culture, we’ve sought out the best in upcoming bands and live acts to grace The Cavern stage. Expect unique parties, diverse sets and genre-spanning dance music from residents before and after.
